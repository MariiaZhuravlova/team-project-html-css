how it’s made?

  • Every one of our ice creams is sourced from the finest ingredients we can find.
  • Twenty five years ago, we set out to create top-notch ice cream, using the organic milk made on our farm in a little village hidden away amongst the gentle hills of North East Wales.
  • We soon converted a little barn and from it, eagerly worked towards our goal; to make a family of ice creams that would be second to none in their taste and creamy texture.
